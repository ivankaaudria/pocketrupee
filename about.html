About PocketRupee

We are an online instant personal loan platform dedicated to providing convenient, transparent, and secure credit services.

Our Mission

PocketRupee operates as a Lending Service Provider (LSP) in partnership with RBI-registered Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs), facilitating instant personal loans through a cutting-edge digital platform built for full regulatory compliance. All credit products offered through PocketRupee are sourced, processed, and disbursed by its NBFC partners in strict accordance with applicable RBI regulations and guidelines.

The platform enables borrowers to complete the entire loan process from application to agreement confirmation on their mobile device, with no physical paperwork required. Loan amounts range from ₹3,000 to ₹1,20,000, catering to diverse financial requirements.

Basic Requirements

To ensure compliance and fund security, applicants must meet the following basic requirements:

  • Be an Indian citizen aged 18 years or above.
  • Have a good credit background.
  • Provide a valid personal bank account for loan disbursement and repayment purposes.
