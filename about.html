About PocketRupee

We are an online instant personal loan platform dedicated to providing convenient, transparent, and secure credit services.

Our Mission

PocketRupee is an online instant personal loan platform dedicated to providing users with convenient, transparent, and secure credit services. Through a fully digital process, users can complete their loan application and obtain credit support in a short time. The application process is simple and efficient, with no paperwork required.

All steps, from application to loan agreement confirmation, can be completed directly on your mobile phone. PocketRupee offers flexible loan amounts ranging from INR 3,000 to INR 120,000, supporting a variety of financial needs such as travel, shopping, medical emergencies, and business cash flow requirements.

Basic Requirements

To ensure compliance and fund security, applicants must meet the following basic requirements:

  • Be an Indian citizen aged 18 years or above.
  • Have a good credit background.
  • Provide a valid personal bank account for loan disbursement and repayment purposes.
