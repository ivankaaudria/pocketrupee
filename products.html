Loan Products

Flexible personal loans from INR 3,000 to INR 120,000 with transparent fees and quick disbursement.

Product Overview

PocketRupee offers flexible loan amounts and tenures to support a variety of financial needs such as travel, shopping, medical emergencies, and business cash flow requirements.

  • Loan amount: INR 3,000 – INR 120,000
  • Loan tenure: 91 days – 180 days

Fees and Charges

Maximum APR

Not exceeding 35% per year (Daily rate ≈ 0.96%)

Processing Fee

Based on the loan amount and tenure

GST

18% GST is applicable on the processing fee as per Indian laws and policies

See how your loan repayment is calculated with this sample:
