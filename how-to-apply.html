How to Apply

Complete your loan application on your phone in five simple steps. No paperwork required.

Step 1

Download and Install the App

Download and install the PocketRupee App on your device from your app store.

Step 2

Register and Create an Account

Register and create an account using your mobile number. The process is quick and straightforward.

Step 3

Submit KYC Information

Submit the required KYC information, such as identity proof, address proof, PAN card, and other documents as requested in the app.

Step 4

Review and Result

After submission, our system will review your application. The result will be displayed in the app.

Step 5

Sign Agreement and Receive Funds

Once approved and the loan agreement is confirmed, the loan amount will be disbursed directly to your bank account.
